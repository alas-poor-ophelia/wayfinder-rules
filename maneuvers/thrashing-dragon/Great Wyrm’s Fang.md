# Great Wyrm’s Fang

By focusing his will into one of his blades, the Thrashing Dragon disciple wields the power of a mighty great wyrm in the palm of his hand. The initiator must make a ranged attack against a target by throwing a light weapon with a range increment (such as a dagger) or a signature Thrashing Dragon weapon. If this attack is successful, the attack inflicts an additional 9d6 points of damage.

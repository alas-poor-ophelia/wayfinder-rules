# Unbreakable Talons

Using his superior skill and speed, the disciple knows where and how to strike for maximum effectiveness. By striking at the right angle, the disciple’s blades set off discordant harmonies within the target for increased destructive potential. When two-weapon fighting with bladed weapons or with natural attacks that inflict slashing damage, the disciple’s attacks with those weapons inflict an additional 4d6 points of damage for the round and strike as if they were adamantine weapons for the purposes of overcoming damage reduction or sundering items.

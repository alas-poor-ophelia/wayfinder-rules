# Alacrity on Wing

With reflexes honed from many combats, the disciple may react to danger with speed so great he may even attack multiple times while he evades the unlucky attacker. The initiator makes an Acrobatics check opposed by the target’s attack roll. If successful the attack is negated and the initiator may make a melee attack with two wielded weapons or unarmed strikes, each dealing an additional +2d6 points of damage.

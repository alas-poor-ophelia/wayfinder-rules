# Sharpened Talons

As a dragon might drag his claws across a castle wall to hone their edge, the disciple has learned to give his own attacks a deadly edge for his next attack. When two-weapon fighting, the disciple’s attacks with those weapons inflict an additional 2d6 points of damage for the round.

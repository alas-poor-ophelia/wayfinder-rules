# Brutal Dragon’s Stance

A Thrashing Dragon disciple sometimes realizes that the best defense is having a field of lifeless opponents at his feet. By adopting the mannerisms of the rampant dragon, he inflicts heavy, chopping blows that decimate his foes as he wades through their broken forms. While in this stance and wielding two-weapons, the initiator suffers a -2 penalty to AC and gains a damage bonus to all melee attacks and unarmed strikes equal to 2d6 + the initiator’s initiation modifier.

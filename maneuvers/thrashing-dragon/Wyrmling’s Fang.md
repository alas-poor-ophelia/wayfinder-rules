# Wyrmling’s Fang

With a quick flick of his wrist, the disciple hurls a blade deeply into his foe with blinding speed. The initiator must make a ranged attack against a target by throwing a light weapon with a range increment (such as a dagger) or a weapon from an associated Thrashing Dragon weapon group (improvised weapon penalties apply if applicable). If this attack is successful, the attack inflicts an additional 1d6 points of damage.

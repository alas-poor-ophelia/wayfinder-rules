# Dragon Rush

With the ferocity of a dragon, the disciple follows through a successful attack with a vicious kick to his foe to keep punishing the fool who would stand before the might of a Thrashing Dragon. Upon successfully striking a foe as part of an attack, martial strike, or a full attack, he may as a swift action use this maneuver to deliver a vicious kick at his full attack bonus with his unarmed strike. This attack inflicts an additional 3d6 points of damage.

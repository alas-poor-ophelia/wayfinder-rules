# Reverberation of Defeat

As you destroy one foe, his allies feel the weight of his failure, the pain, fear, and feeling of utter defeat cascading down the ties of friendship or belief. You can activate this boost when you reduce an opponent to 0 or lower

hit points

. Each of that opponent’s allies within 30 feet must succeed at a

Will

save (DC 15 + your initiation modifier) or become

shaken

and

sickened

for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier.

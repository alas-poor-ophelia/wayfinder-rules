# Reflected Blade Style

Your will creates a glimmering mirror of your assaults, afflicting two enemies at once. After initiating this boost, the next strike you initiate with an initiation action of 1 standatd action that targets one creature also affects an additional opponent within close range (25 feet + 5 feet per 2 initiator levels), even if that opponent would normally be out of range of the strike. If the strike requires an attack roll, use the same attack roll against both targets.

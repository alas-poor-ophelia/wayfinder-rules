# Blazing Mirror Strike

In a flash of bright death, your weapon transforms into light, passing through your foe’s armor and scorching their eyes. Make a melee touch attack with a weapon you are wielding. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal, and the target must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC 14 + your initiation modifier) or become blinded for a number of rounds equal to your initiator level.

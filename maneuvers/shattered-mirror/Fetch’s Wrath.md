# Fetch’s Wrath

Like an unquiet spirit, you travel through light and reflections, striking down those foolish enough to tempt your wrath. When you initiate this strike, you may teleport to any unoccupied space within medium range (100 feet + 10 feet per initiator level), then make a melee attack. Alternately, you may make a melee attack and then teleport to unoccupied space within medium range. In either case, the attack you make deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 4d6 points of damage.

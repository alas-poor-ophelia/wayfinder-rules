# Left-Hand Strike

A minor twist of reality makes it seem like your strike is two places at once. When you initiate this strike, make a Craft check, using the target’s AC as the DC. Then make a melee attack against the target. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal. If your Craft check succeeded, your target loses its shield bonus to AC against this and other attacks this round, and your attack deals an additional 1d6 points of damage.

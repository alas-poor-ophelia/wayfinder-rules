# Curse of the Smoking Mirror

With an obsidian-edged blow, you reflect an enemy’s malice back onto itself, tainting them from within. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal, and the target must succeed at a Will save (DC 15 + your initiation modifier) or fall prey to a reflected curse for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier. While affected, the target becomes cursed, and whenever it deals damage (by any means) during this time it takes an equal amount of damage of the same type(s). Damage Reduction, resistances, and immunities are applied to this damage as normal.

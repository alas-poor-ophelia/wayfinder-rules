# Shattered Glass Smite

With your will and your weapon, you shatter reality as you would shatter a mirror, shredding your enemies with false glass. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 3d6 points of magical slashing damage, and you create a 30-foot cone of shattered glass originating in the target’s space and aimed in a direction of your choice. This cone deals 3d6 points of magical slashing damage to each creature caught within it. You and the initial target of this strike do not take damage from this cone.

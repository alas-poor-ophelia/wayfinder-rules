# Obsidian Razor Style

Like obsidian, you create a reflection that is beautiful, dark, and deadly at the edge of your weapon. After initiating this boost, you gain an insight bonus equal to your ranks in one Craft skill (chosen when you initiate this boost) on melee damage rolls for one round.

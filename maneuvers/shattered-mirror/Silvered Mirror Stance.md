# Silvered Mirror Stance

Like a prism or a silver mirror, you splash joy and light onto those whom you love. When you enter this stance, choose a single power, psi-like ability, spell, or spell-like ability with a range greater than personal affecting you. While you maintain this stance, allies within 30 feet of you also gain the benefits of the chosen effect for as long as it is affecting you.

# Touch of the Witch

Your weapons become charged with malicious energies. While you maintain this stance, any creature you hit with a melee weapon (including natural weapons) becomes cursed for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier. This stance’s effect resolves after other effects relating to the attack (such as strikes or weapon properties).

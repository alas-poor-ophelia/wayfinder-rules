# Persecution

This strike amplifies the misfortune already afflicting an enemy, turning their curse into deep wounds. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 4d6 points of damage. If the target is cursed, this strike’s additional damage increases to 8d8.

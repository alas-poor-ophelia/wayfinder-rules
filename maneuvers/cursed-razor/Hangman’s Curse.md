# Hangman’s Curse

This grim curse afflicts its victims with a breathless exhaustion, and a dark bruise forms around their necks. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal, and the target must succeed at a

Fortitude

save (DC 14 + your initiation modifier) or become cursed and

exhausted

for a number of rounds equal to your initiator level.

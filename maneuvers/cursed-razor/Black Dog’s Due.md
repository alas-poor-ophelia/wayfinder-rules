# Black Dog’s Due

You strike a distracted foe, bringing swift death with the edge of your blade. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 8d6 points of damage, and the target must succeed at a

Reflex

save (DC 17 + your initiation modifier) or be knocked

prone

. If the target is

flanked

(either by you or by other creatures), this strike’s additional damage increases to 12d6.

# Torment the Weak

This vicious strike rends its victims and causes those under your dark power to vent their life force. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 1d6 points of damage. If the target is cursed, it takes an additional 1d6 points of damage each round at the start of its turn for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier.

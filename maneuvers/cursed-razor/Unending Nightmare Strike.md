# Unending Nightmare Strike

This cruel and unusual strike traps its victims in a waking nightmare. Make a melee attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus 4 points of damage to the target’s

Intelligence

,

Wisdom

, and

Charisma

scores. At the beginning of each of your turns thereafter, the target must succeed at a

Will

save (DC 18 + your initiation modifier) or take an additional 4 points of damage to its

Intelligence

,

Wisdom

, and

Charisma

scores. A successful save or the creature’s death ends this effect.

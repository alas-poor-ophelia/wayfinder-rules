# Luck Shifting

One man’s ill luck is another man’s fortune. After initiating this boost, you gain a +1

luck bonus

to your AC until the start of your next turn for each cursed opponent within medium range (100 feet + 10 feet per initiator level), up to a maximum bonus of +5.

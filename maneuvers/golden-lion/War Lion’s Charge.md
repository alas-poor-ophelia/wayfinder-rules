# War Lion’s Charge

With the ferocity of an angered lion, the Golden Lion disciple races across the battlefield ignoring secondary targets to bear down on a single enemy and reduce him to dust. The initiator makes a charge attack against a foe (which does not provoke attacks of opportunity) and this attack inflicts an additional 14d6 points of damage. The foe must also make a Fortitude save (DC 17 + initiation modifier) or be stunned for one round.

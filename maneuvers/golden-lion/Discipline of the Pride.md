# Discipline of the Pride

Through training with his allies, the disciple has taught them the basics of his military skill that they may call upon in times of duress. As a swift action, the initiator may grant one of his combat feats to any of his allies within 30-ft. for 3 rounds. No initiator can benefit from this ability more than once at a time.

# Triumphant Lion’s Leadership

A beacon of victory upon the field of combat, the disciple personifies glory in combat and those in his presence may share in his prowess. The initiator and any allies within 10-ft. of the disciple gains an additional +1d6 points of damage per attack per foe that the initiator has slain (maximum of five slain foes for +5d6 bonus damage) while this stance is in effect.

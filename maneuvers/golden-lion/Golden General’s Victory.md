# Golden General’s Victory

The power and majesty of the lion lords of the wild lives within the disciple, so much so that his victories are a victory for all of his allies. When the initiator reduces a foe to 0 hit points or below he may initiate this boost; his allies are then bolstered and their hearts are rededicated to victory. All allies within 60-ft. of the initiator have a number of hit points equal to the disciple’s initiator level restored.

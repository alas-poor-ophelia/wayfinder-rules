# Distracting Strike

By attracting the attentions of a foe with a well- placed and vicious blow, the disciple of the Golden Lion may distract and weaken his foe to the predations of his allies. Upon a successful attack when using this strike, the initiator inflicts an additional 2d6 points of damage and the target creature is left flat-footed until its next turn. Creatures immune to critical hits are not left flat-footed.

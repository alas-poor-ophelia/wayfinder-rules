# Guard the Pride

By trusting the awareness of the celestial realm, the discipline trusts in his heavenly sense of the battle around him to see where the next attack is coming and move away from danger. As an immediate action, the initiator grants a circumstance bonus to the target adjacent ally’s armor class for one round equal to half the disciple’s initiator level.

# Golden General’s Attitude

A true Golden Lion disciple is a beacon of command on the field of battle, inspiring his troops by his mere presence. All allies within 60-ft. of the initiator gain a +1 morale bonus to attack and damage rolls, and to saves vs fear effects per four initiator levels.

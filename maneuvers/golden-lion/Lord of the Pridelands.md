# Lord of the Pridelands

Proving he is the undisputed master of war, the Golden Lion master calls for absolute and crushing victory through overwhelming force of numbers. This ability is greatly feared by the master’s enemies, because this can turn even the most green of soldier into a capable warrior. For every ally within the 60- ft. range of this boost, the initiator and each of these allies gains a +2 morale bonus to hit, damage, AC, and saving throws for one round (to a maximum of +10).

# Riddle of Iron

With a flourishing of his blade, the Scarlet Throne disciple confuses the senses of his foe as he lands a strike from an unexpected angle. The initiator must make a melee attack against a foe, and if successful the strike inflicts an additional 5d6 points of damage and dazes the target for one round on a failed Will save (DC 15 + initiation modifier).

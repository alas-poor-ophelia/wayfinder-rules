# Unfettered Movement

The Scarlet Throne disciple is swift in battle and in his ability to go from one skirmish to the next to choose his next foe. By assuming this stance, the disciple gains a +10-ft. bonus to his base speed and gains a +4 dodge bonus to his armor class against attacks of opportunity.

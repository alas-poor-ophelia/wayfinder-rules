# Scarlet Duelist Attitude

The force of will that a disciple of Scarlet Throne possesses is enough to protect him and guide his steps through battle, and his battle focus allows him to anticipate danger before it happens. By adopting this stance, the disciple may add +5 insight bonus to his Armor Class, CMD, and to his Initiative score.

# Regal Blade

A disciple of the Scarlet Throne learns that in moments of precise focus, a single stroke of the blade can fell a potent enemy. The disciple may add a +2 insight bonus to his attack roll and +1d8 insight bonus to his damage roll on a single melee attack made during this turn.

# Black Wind

You unleash a mighty shot that thunders through enemy lines, knocking aside any in its path. When you initiate this maneuver, you create a 50-foot line that deals 15d6 points of damage. Creatures caught in the line can make a

Reflex

save (DC 17 + your initiation modifier) to take half damage. Any creature that fails its

Reflex

save must also succeed at a

Fortitude

save with the same DC or become

dazed

for one round.

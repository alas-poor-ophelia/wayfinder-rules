# Steel Tornado

Your masterful shot spins your enemy around with incredible force, causing them to lose their grip and their balance. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 10d6 points of damage, and you can make a

trip

attempt against the target as a

free action

. In addition, you can make a

disarm

attempt against the creature for each item it is holding or wielding. These

combat maneuver

attempts use a Sleight of Hand check in place of your

combat maneuver

check, and do not provoke

attacks of opportunity

.

# Distracting Draft

Your well placed shot can both hurt the enemy and put them off their guard. Make a ranged

feint

attempt against a creature you can see, using a

Sleight of Hand

check in place of the normal

Bluff

check. If your

feint

is successful, your next ranged attack against that target within one round deals an additional 1d6 points of damage.

# Dustcatching Breeze

You target your enemy with unscrupulous tactics, throwing them off balance and allowing your allies to seize the advantage. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 2d6 points of damage, and you can make a

dirty trick

attempt against the target as a

free action

, using a Sleight of Hand check in place of your

combat maneuver

check.

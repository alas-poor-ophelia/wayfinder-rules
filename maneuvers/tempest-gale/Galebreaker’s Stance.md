# Galebreaker’s Stance

Your ability to accurately read the wind allows you to fight effectively even in the most adverse conditions. While you maintain this stance, you do not take any penalties on ranged attacks due to high wind speed, and your ranged attacks ignore the

wind wall

spell and similar magical wind effects that would negatively affect them.

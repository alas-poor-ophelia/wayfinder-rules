# Exploding Gale

Even those who hide behind their fellows cannot save themselves from your deadly shots. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 4d6 points of damage, and sparks and shrapnel explode from the attack, dealing damage to creatures adjacent to the target equal to your initiation modifier. Creatures other than the primary target can make a

Reflex

save (DC 14 + your initiation modifier) to avoid the damage.

# Winds of Vengeance Stance

Every move that your enemies make is an opportunity for you, and one that you relish taking advantage of. While you maintain this stance, you can make a number of additional

attacks of opportunity

each round equal to your initiation modifier. This stacks with the Combat Reflexes feat and other effects that increase the number of

attacks of opportunity

you can make in a round. In addition, any time an enemy hits an ally within 60 feet of you with an attack, you can make an attack of opportunity against that enemy using a ranged weapon you are wielding.

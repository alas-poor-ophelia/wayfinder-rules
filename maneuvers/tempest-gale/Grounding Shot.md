# Grounding Shot

Your slings and arrows can bring down even the mightiest flyers, dropping them to the earth where their wings mean nothing. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 5d6 points of damage, and if the target of the attack is flying, they must succeed at a

Fly

check opposed by your Sleight of Hand check or lose their ability to fly for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier, falling to the ground and taking falling damage as normal.

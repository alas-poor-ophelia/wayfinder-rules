# Sniper’s Eye Stance

As a tempest gale disciple, distance is no obstacle to you. While you maintain this stance, you reduce the total penalty from attacking creatures beyond your weapon’s first range increment by 2, and you gain a +2 bonus on

combat maneuver

checks made with ranged attacks. For every four initiator levels you possess, the penalty to ranged attacks lessens by another 2, and the bonus to ranged

combat maneuver

checks increases by +2.

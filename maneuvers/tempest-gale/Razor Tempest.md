# Razor Tempest

A scything wind seems to follow your attacks, leaving gashes that

bleed

profusely in the flesh of your enemy. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 8d6 points of damage and

bleed

damage equal to your initiation modifier. The target continues to

bleed

until the bleeding is stopped via magical

healing

or a successful DC 15

Heal

check.

# Blinding Shot

With a skilled shot, you take your opponent’s sight, and with it, the threat they pose. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 5d6 points of damage, and the target must succeed at a

Fortitude

save (DC 15 + your initiation modifier) or become

blinded

for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier.

# Piercing Shot

The power behind your ranged attacks is such that they can strike through one foe to pierce another behind them. When you initiate this strike, you create a 30-foot line that deals 3d6 points of damage. Creatures caught in the line can make a

Reflex

save (DC 12 + your initiation modifier) to take half damage.

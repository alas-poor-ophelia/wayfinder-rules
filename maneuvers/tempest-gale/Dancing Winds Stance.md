# Dancing Winds Stance

The wind whips around you as you move, guarding you against attacks so long as you remain in motion. While you maintain this stance, in any round that you move at least 10 feet, you gain the benefits of the

improved evasion

ability and attacks made against you suffer a 20% miss chance until the start of your next turn.

# Hunting Zephyr

As a master of the Tempest Gale, you never miss your mark; each shot is perfect. Make a ranged attack. This attack automatically hits, threatens a

critical hit

, and cannot be negated by a counter or other defensive ability activated as an

immediate action

(such as the emergency force sphere spell or a similar effect). It deals weapon damage as normal.

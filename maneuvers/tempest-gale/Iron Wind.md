# Iron Wind

A single powerful blow can leave an enemy reeling, and you excel at delivering such blows. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deal weapon damage as normal plus an additional 4d6 points of damage, and the target must succeed at a

Fortitude

save (DC 14 + your initiation modifier) or become

staggered

for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier.

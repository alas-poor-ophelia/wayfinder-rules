# False Wind

You unleash a rapid shot that distracts or disables an enemy, preventing them from harming you or your ally. You can initiate this counter in response to an attack being made against you or an ally by a creature you can see. Make a ranged attack against that creature. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal, and the ally targeted by the creature you attacked can move up to their speed as a

free action

, even if it isn’t their turn. This movement does not provoke

attacks of opportunity

.

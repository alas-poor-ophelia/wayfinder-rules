# Breath Stealing Wind

You fire a single shot that steals the very breath from his target. Make a ranged attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 10d6 points of damage, and the target must succeed at a

Fortitude

save (DC 18 + your initiation modifier) or become silenced (as the spell) for a number of rounds equal to your initiation modifier.

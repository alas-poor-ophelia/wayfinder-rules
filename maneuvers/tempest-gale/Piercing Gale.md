# Piercing Gale

No foe can withstand the force of your weapons. When you initiate this maneuver, you create a 50-foot line that deals 10d6 points of damage. Creatures caught in the line can make a

Reflex

save (DC 15 + your initiation modifier) to take half damage. Any creature that fails its save is also knocked

prone

by the force of the attack.

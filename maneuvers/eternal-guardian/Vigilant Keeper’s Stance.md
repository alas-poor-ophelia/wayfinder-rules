# Vigilant Keeper’s Stance

You adopt an aggressive stance which slows your enemies’ ability to advance or retreat. While you maintain this stance, opponents treat all squares you threaten as difficult terrain. This does not prevent those opponents from charging through your

threatened

area, however. In addition, whenever you hit an opponent with an

attack of opportunity

, that opponent becomes cursed until the end of your next turn.

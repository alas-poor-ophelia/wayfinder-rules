# Jailer of the Damned

You adopt the stance of the wardens which guard the gates of death. While you maintain this stance, your maneuvers and other abilities treat creatures that are normally immune to

mind-affecting

effects and

fear

effects as if they were not immune. Such creatures gain a +5

resistance bonus

on saving throws against these effects, rather than ignoring them outright. In addition, while you maintain this stance, you do not provoke

attacks of opportunity

when attempting a combat maneuver against a creature that is

shaken

,

frightened

, or

panicked

, and you gain a +2 bonus on your combat maneuver checks against such creatures.

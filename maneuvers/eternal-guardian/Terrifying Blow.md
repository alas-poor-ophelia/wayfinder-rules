# Terrifying Blow

With a bellowing cry, you deliver a fearsome wound to your opponent. Make an attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal, and the target must succeed at a

Will

save (DC 11 + your initiation modifier) or become

frightened

for one round. If your target is cursed, your attack deals an additional 1d6 points of damage.

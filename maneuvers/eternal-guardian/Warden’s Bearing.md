# Warden’s Bearing

You shift your weight and assume a stronger, more impressive position. You can activate this boost while making a

combat maneuver

attempt. You gain a +2 bonus on your

combat maneuver

check, you do not provoke

attacks of opportunity

for making that

combat maneuver

attempt, and you are treated as one size category larger for purposes of determining if your

combat maneuver

can affect your target (such as a Medium creature attempting to

bull rush

a Huge creature).

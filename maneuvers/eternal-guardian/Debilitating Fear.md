# Debilitating Fear

You fill your target with the fear that prey feels when it sees a mighty predator, impairing their ability to act. When you activate this boost, choose one creature within range that is

cowering

,

shaken

,

frightened

, or

panicked

. That creature must succeed at a

Will

save (DC 12 + your initiation modifier) or become

dazed

for 1 round.

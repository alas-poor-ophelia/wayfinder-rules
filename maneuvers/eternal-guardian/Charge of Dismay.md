# Charge of Dismay

The disciple barrels through the enemy lines, terrifying his foes. Make a charge attack that does not provoke

attacks of opportunity

. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus an additional 6d6 points of damage, and all opponents (including the target) within 30 feet must succeed at a

Will

save (DC 16 + your initiation modifier) or become

frightened

for 1d4 rounds and

shaken

for one minute after. Creatures with more hit dice than your initiator level cannot become

frightened

by this strike, although they still become

shaken

on a failed save. Any creature that starts its turn without line of sight to you can make another saving throw to end this effect.

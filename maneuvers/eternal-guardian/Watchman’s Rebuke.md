# Watchman’s Rebuke

Your

supernatural

will calls for your enemies to cease resisting and submit to your judgement. You can initiate this counter when an opponent within 60 feet succeeds on a saving throw. That opponent must immediately reroll its save, using the result of the second roll to determine if it succeeded or failed. If that opponent is cursed, it takes a –2 penalty on this reroll.

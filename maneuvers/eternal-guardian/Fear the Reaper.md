# Fear the Reaper

You have learned to use the fear you create as a method of defense for both yourself and your allies. You can initiate this counter in response to a melee or ranged attack being made against an ally (including you) within 60 feet. Make an

Intimidate

check, using your opponent’s

attack roll

as the DC. If you succeed, the attack is negated, and the attacker becomes

shaken

for one round, as if you had demoralized them with the

Intimidate

check.

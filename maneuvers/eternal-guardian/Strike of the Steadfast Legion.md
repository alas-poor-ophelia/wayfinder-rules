# Strike of the Steadfast Legion

When you bring your enemy low, your strength bolsters the will of your allies. Make an attack. If it hits, it deals weapon damage as normal plus additional damage equal to 1/2 your initiator level, and all allies within 60 feet of you gain

damage reduction

/– equal to your initiation modifier (minimum 1) for one round. In addition, any ally who gains

damage reduction

from this strike can expend a number of hit point up to your initiator level to grant any other ally who gained damage reduction from this strike three times that amount in

temporary hit points

. These

temporary hit points

last for one round.

# Stand Your Ground

You and your ally stand together to defend your position, strengthened by

supernatural

resolve. When you initate this counter, choose an ally within 60 feet. For one round, you and that ally each take only half the damage you would otherwise take. Damage that is redirected (such as by the

shield other

spell or the share pain power) or intentionally taken (such as by strike of the steadfast legion) is not reduced.

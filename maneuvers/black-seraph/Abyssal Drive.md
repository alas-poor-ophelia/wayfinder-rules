# Abyssal Drive

The path of the conqueror demands satisfaction, and the disciple of the Black Seraph learns perfection in conquest by the ever progressing tide of battle. Pressing forward with infernally enhanced power, the disciple moves to devastate his next foe with the rage within his soul. The disciple makes a charge attack which does not provoke attacks of opportunity, and this inflicts an additional 8d6 points of damage and leaves his foe sickened for 1d4 rounds. If the victim successfully makes a Fortitude save (DC 16 + initiation modifier), he negates the sickened condition. When making a charge attack with this strike, he does not suffer the standard -2 Armor Class penalty.

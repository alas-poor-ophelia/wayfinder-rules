# Sharing the Dark Soul

By channeling their own internal despair and anguish, the disciple shares this inner pain with his enemies. A burst of crackling energy accompanies the strike as the inner fiend reaches out and touches the mind of its victim. The initiator makes an attack which deals an additional 8d6 points of profane damage, and the foe is dazed with pain for one round on a failed Fortitude save (DC 15 + initiation modifier negates dazed condition).

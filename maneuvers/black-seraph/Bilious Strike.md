# Bilious Strike

With a powerful strike to the abdomen, the Black Seraph disciple fills his opponent with destructive unholy power. The disciple’s aim is to rupture organs within to kill his subject painfully. The initiator must make a melee attack against a target creature, and if successful the attack inflicts an additional 6d6 points of profane damage and nauseates the target for the disciple’s initiation modifier in rounds. The target may make a Fortitude save (DC 14 + initiation modifier) to reduce the nauseated condition to one round.

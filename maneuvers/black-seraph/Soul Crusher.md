# Soul Crusher

With a mighty swing or the barest tap, the rage and hatred within the Black Seraph infects his foe and destroys his resolve and very will to live. The initiator makes a melee attack against a target creature, and if successful the attack inflicts an additional 3d4 points of Charisma damage as the essence of the target’s self is devoured by the disciple’s hatred for his existence.

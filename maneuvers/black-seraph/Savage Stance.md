# Savage Stance

The blood is the life and the disciple of the Black Seraph knows that by spilling it in great amounts, he does glory to the lower planes. While in this stance and wielding a melee weapon in two hands, the disciple adds an additional 1d8 points of damage to his attacks but suffers a -2 to attack rolls from the focus on increased damage. Upon reaching initiator level 9, this bonus increases to 2d8 points of damage, and increases to 3d8 upon reaching initiator level 17.

# Consumption Strike

By channeling dark energies from the lower planes, the disciple of the Black Seraph may utilize this vampiric power through his attack, restoring health and strength to him at the cost of his foe’s vitality. This strike deals an additional 10d6 points of profane damage, and the disciple gains a number of temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt by this strike. These hit points persist for up to one minute or until they are lost.

# Inner Demon Strike

By using the black energies within him as a weapon, the disciple may channel it through his mortal form to do devastating damage to his foes, all the while heedless of the damage inflicted upon his body. This black energy inflicts an additional 4d6 points of profane damage, which also deals 1d6 points of damage to the initiator.

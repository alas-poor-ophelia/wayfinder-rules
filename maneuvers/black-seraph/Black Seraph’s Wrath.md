# Black Seraph’s Wrath

The disciple of the Black Seraph’s anger and wrath are his strongest weapons, and in concentrating this hatred into his attacks, he may cause a horrible black power to invade the wounds of his foes to cause even more injury. The initiator makes an Intimidate check and the result of this check is added to his damage roll as profane damage on his next successful attack.

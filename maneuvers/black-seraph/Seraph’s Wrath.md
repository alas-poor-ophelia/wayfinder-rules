# Seraph’s Wrath

The Black Seraph disciple uses the principles of overwhelming force to drive his opponent back so that he may continue to wade forward towards conquest. The disciple makes a melee attack against a target creature that inflicts an additional 2d6 points of damage and causes the target to make a Reflex save (DC 12 + initiation modifier) or be knocked prone.

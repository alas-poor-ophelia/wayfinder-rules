# Void Seraph Strike

The Black Seraph disciple’s power and mastery is nearly complete, and with this maneuver he may dismiss his hatred and set it upon a foe to devour the fool who would stand before him. The initiator must make a ranged touch attack against a target creature, and if successful the target is ripped and slashed by the claws and wings of shadowy birds of venomous hate, taking 6d6 points of profane damage per round for 1d4 rounds. While being devoured by these shadowy birds, the target suffers a 50% miss chance on any attacks he makes due to his senses being obscured by the flapping wings and horrible pain he is enduring.

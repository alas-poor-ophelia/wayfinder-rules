# Ravaging Blow

Fear is one of the main weapons in the arsenal of a Black Seraph disciple, and by using it he may shake his opponent to their core. The disciple makes a melee attack that inflicts an additional 1d6 points of profane damage and may cause the victim to be shaken for one round due to his fearful countenance and martial prowess. A successfulWill save (DC 11 + initiation modifier) negates the shaken effect.

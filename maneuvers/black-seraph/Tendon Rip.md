# Tendon Rip

By striking at the legs of the opponent, the disciple removes the mobility of a foe, so the foe cannot hamper his progression to victory. The initiator makes an attack on the target creature, and if successful, the attack inflicts an additional 2d6 points of damage and halves the opponent’s move speed for initiation modifier in rounds.

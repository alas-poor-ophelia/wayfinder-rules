# Emerald-Tipped Feathers

A defensive technique of the Silver Crane calls for the hardening of the channeled celestial’s essence around the disciple to protect him from harm. This essence often manifests in the form of a wing-like shield of faintly green light, which grants you damage reduction of 20/evil against a single attack.

# Silver Crane’s Leap

The flow of battle runs sharply through the Silver Crane disciple’s mind, allowing him to see where his opponents will move before he makes his own. By watching these flows, he may step before their movements, allowing him greater mobility without a cost to defense. The disciple may make a free movement up to his normal movement speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.

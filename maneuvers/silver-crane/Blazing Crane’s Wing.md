# Blazing Crane’s Wing

By filling the Silver Crane disciple’s weapon with the strength of his celestial patrons, the warrior funnels their righteous wrath against those that are abominations in the eyes of all that is good. The initiator’s attacks until his next turn inflict an additional 2d6 points of damage against undead or evil outsiders.

# Silver Crane Resurgence

By invoking the power of his celestial patron, the Silver Crane disciple may overcome the fell powers and curses of his enemies and return to full fighting ability. As an immediate action, the initiator may grant himself or an ally within 30-ft of his position the ability to re-roll a failed saving throw with a +4 sacred bonus.

# Silver Crane’s Mercy

With a recitation of power and a prayer for mercy upon those who are servants of heaven, the Silver Crane disciple strikes out at the evil he battles. Upon making a successful attack, you or an ally within 30-ft of you is healed of 9d6 points of damage.

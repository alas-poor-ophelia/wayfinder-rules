# Argent King’s Scepter

The initiator of this strike holds his weapon aloft briefly, allowing it to fill with holy wrath before striking the blazing weapon down with righteous finality. This strike inflicts an additional 12d6 points of damage and heals the initiator for 60 points of damage.

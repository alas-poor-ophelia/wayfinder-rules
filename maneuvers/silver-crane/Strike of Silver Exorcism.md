# Strike of Silver Exorcism

The ultimate technique of the Silver Crane is a strike that rends the essence of the foes of the heavens asunder and brings them to ruin in one blazing stroke. This strike adds an additional 80 points of sacred damage to the attack and dazzles the foe for one round. Undead or evil outsiders who are struck by this attack suffer 120 points of sacred damage instead and must make a Will save (DC 19 + initiation modifier) or be instantly slain. Creatures that succeed on the saving throw are instead hindered by holy power, being under the effects of a slow spell for the character’s initiation modifier in rounds as their very essence quakes with the agonies inflicted by pure celestial energies.

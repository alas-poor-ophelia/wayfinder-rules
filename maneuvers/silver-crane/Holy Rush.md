# Holy Rush

With a rush of speed and flare of arching wings of light, the martial disciple speeds towards an ally within the blink of an eye. When initiating this boost, you teleport to an square within 50 ft. and adjacent to an ally. If there is no ally within this range, this maneuver cannot be used. The disciple may teleport himself, his gear and equipment, and up to 25 pounds of carried objects.
